Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

We only collect the data necessary for the application to function:

  • Registration data: name, email, and password (or data provided by Google/Apple Sign In);
  • Profile picture: optional, provided by the user or imported from the Google/Apple account;
  • Progress data: answers to questions, practice streak, completed sessions, and daily focus;
  • Device data: push notification token and timezone;
  • Subscription data: plan type and validity, processed by Apple/Google and RevenueCat.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and personalize the study experience;
  • Save your progress and statistics;
  • Send practice reminder notifications (configurable);
  • Manage your Premium subscription;
  • Improve the application based on aggregated and anonymous data.

3. Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase (cloud infrastructure). Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text. Communication between the application and the servers is protected by TLS/HTTPS encryption.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. Your data may be processed by the following infrastructure providers, exclusively for service operation:

  • Supabase: database and authentication;
  • RevenueCat: subscription management;
  • Expo (Expo Push Service): push notification delivery;
  • Apple/Google: social authentication and payment processing.

5. Your Rights

In accordance with the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD — Law 13.709/2018) and equivalent international regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data;
  • Correct incomplete or outdated data;
  • Request deletion of your data;
  • Withdraw consent for data processing;
  • Request data portability.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at dailyvetapp@gmail.com.

6. Push Notifications

DailyVet may send push notifications to remind you to practice. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in the application or device settings.

7. Minors

DailyVet is intended for users who are at least 14 years of age. We do not intentionally collect data from minors under 14. If we become aware that we have collected data from a minor under 14, we will delete such data immediately.

8. Cookies and Tracking

DailyVet does not use cookies, third-party tracking, or advertising tools. We do not display ads within the application.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. We recommend that you review it regularly. Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notification.
